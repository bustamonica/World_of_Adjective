Artist Statement

Food, water, father, mother, brother, sister, daughter, friend, forest, rain, house, career, country, things. We live in a world of nouns. Defining everything with nouns is the first step of understanding them. But is nouns really indispensable? Or is adjectives indispensable? What if we live in a world not made of nouns but of adjectives? How will it affect our perception of the world?

The project tries to construct a world made only of adjectives. Starting from the first image, each one following is chosen in relation to the previous one. To view the dictionary in order, click on jesus and press right arrow to go to the next one.
